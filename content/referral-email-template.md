# Referral Activation Email Template

Send individually to 20 past clients and warm contacts. Personalize each one — do not mass-send.

---

## Template

**Subject:** Quick update + a question

Hi [Name],

Hope you're well. I wanted to share something I've been working on.

Over the past couple of years, I've formalized the approach I've been developing into the CLEAR Change Framework — a structured methodology for organizational change that's grounded in behavioral psychology and systems thinking. It's the distillation of everything I've learned working with organizations on transformation, leadership development, and behavioral design.

I've built it out at clear-framework.com if you're curious.

I'm currently taking on new consulting engagements — change management, leadership development, psychometric assessments — and I'm wondering: **is there anyone in your network who might benefit from this kind of work?**

The sweet spot is organizations going through a significant change (restructuring, merger, culture shift, scaling) where the human side of the equation is being underserved. Or leadership teams that want development grounded in psychology rather than generic frameworks.

If someone comes to mind, I'd really appreciate a warm introduction. Happy to share whatever context would be helpful.

Thanks — and genuinely great to reconnect.

Erik

---

## Personalization Notes

- If you worked with them recently: reference the specific project
- If it's been a while: add a line asking what they're working on
- If they're in a specific industry: mention that industry as an example
- If they're senior: ask if their own organization might benefit, not just their network
- Always use their first name, never "Dear [Name]"

## Follow-up (if no response after 7 days)

**Subject:** Re: Quick update + a question

Hi [Name], just bumping this in case it got buried. No pressure at all — if anyone comes to mind, I'd appreciate the connection. And if not, hope you're doing well regardless.

Erik

## Tracking

Record in the spreadsheet:
- Date sent
- Recipient name + org
- Response (Y/N)
- Introduction made (Y/N)
- Lead quality (if applicable)
